Privacy Policy for GSweeper Chrome Extension

Introduction

GSweeper is a Chrome extension designed to help users efficiently manage their email subscriptions by enabling bulk unsubscribes and email deletions. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to ensuring that your data is handled securely and transparently. This Privacy Policy outlines how GSweeper collects, uses, and protects your information.

Data Collection

GSweeper does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside of your browser. All operations, such as identifying and managing subscription emails, occur locally on your device.

Information GSweeper Accesses:

  • Email Metadata: GSweeper accesses metadata (e.g., sender email addresses, subject lines) to identify subscription emails.
  • Email Content: The extension may scan email content locally to determine eligibility for unsubscribing or deletion. This data is not stored or transmitted.

What GSweeper Does Not Collect:

  • GSweeper does not collect or transmit your personal information, email content, or any identifiable data to external servers.
  • GSweeper does not use analytics tools, trackers, or any third-party services to monitor your activity.

Data Usage

All data accessed by GSweeper is used solely for the purpose of providing the extension's core functionality:

  • Identifying marketing emails.
  • Managing unsubscribes and deletions.
  • Improving inbox storage management.

No data is shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Data Security

GSweeper ensures your data remains secure by:

  • Operating entirely within your browser.
  • Not transmitting or storing data externally.
  • Using secure APIs (e.g., Gmail API) in compliance with Google's policies to execute operations.

User Control

  • Permissions: GSweeper requests the minimum necessary permissions to access your Gmail account and perform the extension’s functionality.
  • Revocation: You can revoke GSweeper’s access at any time through your Google Account settings.

GDPR Compliance

GSweeper is designed to comply with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations by:

  • Not processing or storing personal data outside your local environment.
  • Offering full transparency about how the extension operates.
  • Allowing users to uninstall the extension and remove any local data it uses.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how GSweeper handles your data, you can contact us at:

  • Email: support@gsweeper.com
  • Website: https://gsweeper.com

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or changes in compliance requirements. Users will be notified of significant updates, and the latest version will always be available here.

By using GSweeper, you agree to this Privacy Policy and its terms. Your trust is essential to us, and we remain dedicated to safeguarding your data while enhancing your email management experience.
